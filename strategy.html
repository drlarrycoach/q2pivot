The Async-First
Timetable Audit

"Moving lecture delivery to async AI allows for a high-intensity 4-day synthesis week."

The Challenge

Traditional 15-week semesters are designed for information scarcity. In an age of information abundance, the "lecture" is the least efficient use of expensive campus real estate.

The Pivot

Condense rote content delivery into AI-driven asynchronous modules. Reclaim campus hours for high-value research, mentorship, and co-creation. Moving to a 4-day "Synthesis Week" increases faculty and student wellbeing.

Strategic Timeline

  • 01 Utilization Audit: Analyze campus usage against pedagogical outcomes.
  • 02 Async Migration: Repurposing 101-level content for AI avatars.
  • 03 Institutional Roadmap: Moving the provost's office toward Q2 Pivot.
